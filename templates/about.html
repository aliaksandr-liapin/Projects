About SmartHouse

Smart Living, Simplified

At SmartHouse, we believe your home should work for you—seamlessly, securely, and sustainably. Our platform empowers you to control, monitor, and optimize your smart devices from one intuitive dashboard, making everyday life effortless.

Founded in 2024, SmartHouse began as a small team of tech enthusiasts tired of juggling multiple smart home apps. Today, we're a trusted solution for thousands of users who value:

Simplicity

One dashboard for all devices.

Security

End-to-end encryption for cameras and data.

Customization

Adaptable to apartments, homes, and offices.
