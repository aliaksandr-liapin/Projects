Frequently Asked Questions

What is SmartHouse?

SmartHouse is a comprehensive smart home platform that allows you to control and monitor all your smart devices from a single dashboard.

How do I get started?

Simply create an account, connect your devices, and start controlling your smart home through our intuitive dashboard.

Which devices are compatible?

We support a wide range of smart devices including lights, thermostats, cameras, door locks, and more from major manufacturers.

How do I add new devices?

Use the "Add Device" button in your dashboard and follow the step-by-step setup process for your specific device.

How secure is my data?

We use end-to-end encryption and follow industry best practices to ensure your data remains secure and private.

Can I control who accesses my home?

Yes, you can set up multiple user accounts with different access levels and permissions.

What if my device is not responding?

Try restarting the device, checking your internet connection, or resetting the device connection in the dashboard.

How do I get support?

Contact our support team at support@ezsmarthouse.com or use the in-app support feature.
