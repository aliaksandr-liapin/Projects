Sensor Setup Guide

Getting Started

This guide will help you set up your smart sensors and connect them to your SmartHouse dashboard. Follow these steps carefully to ensure proper installation and functionality.

1

Prepare Your Sensors

  • Unpack all sensor components
  • Check for any physical damage
  • Ensure batteries are properly installed
  • Verify sensor compatibility with your devices
2

Installation Locations

Temperature Sensors
  • • Place away from direct sunlight
  • • Keep away from heat sources
  • • Install at average room height
Motion Sensors
  • • Mount at 6-8 feet height
  • • Avoid direct sunlight
  • • Clear line of sight
3

Connect to Dashboard

  1. Open the SmartHouse dashboard
  2. Click "Add Device" in the top menu
  3. Select "Sensor" from the device type menu
  4. Follow the on-screen pairing instructions
  5. Test sensor functionality

Troubleshooting Tips

Common Issues
  • Sensor not connecting? Try resetting the device
  • Inaccurate readings? Check sensor placement
  • Battery issues? Replace with fresh batteries
